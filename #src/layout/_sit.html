What is an SIT?

In order to buy athletes'/clubs' tokens on the platform, users deal with SIT, which can be traded on crypto-exchanges.

The token is launched on the Binance Smart Chain (BEP20) and has already been verified by the Binance Smart Chain Explorer validator.

In the third quarter of 2021 the token is to become multi-chain and is going to be available on such blockchains as Freeton, Ethereum and Tron. It is also going to feature our own product "SIT connecting bridge".
