OUR PLANS

The Roadmap

  • 2019
    Beginning stages of development of the platform and the token. Team building.
  • 01.2021 – 14.08.2021
    Development of the technical and blockchain parts of the platform. Adding 5,000 players and 215 clubs to the platform
  • 15.08.2021 – 30.09.2021
    • listing on Pancake Swap and other exchanges
    • platform release
    • launch of a marketing campaign in 15 countries
    • collaboration with global ambassadors
  • 1.10.2021 – 31.12.2021
    • mobile app release
    • assigning tokens to basketball and hockey teams and athletes based on ratings
    • centralised exchanges entry (Asia, small exchanges)
  • 1.01.2022 – 31.03.2022
    • top cryptocurrency exchanges entry
    • launch of tokens assigned to boxers, MMA fighters, athletes and cricket teams
  • 1.04.2022 – 30.06.2022
    • launch of our own fully-functioning players’ token exchange
    • retired athletes’ NFT- token release
  • 1.07.2022 – 30.09.2022
    • gamification of the platform in the form of Fantasy Sport
    • NFT-auction launch and player's cards release
    • launch of a decentralised football club
  • 30.09.2022 – ∞
    • global partnerships with sports organisations
    • conclusion of contracts with clubs, players and corporations
    • development of the SIT football fund

Our roadmap focuses on the development of the technology, operational infrastructure and new partnerships.
